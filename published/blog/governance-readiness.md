Leadership teams rarely lack **policies** — they lack **clarity** on whether those policies match what regulators expect *now*.

### The gap

Most organisations run manual reviews once a year. Meanwhile, guidance shifts, models change, and shadow IT grows.

### A different slice

Upload your AI policy, map it to a structured checklist, and surface **gaps and actions** — not another generic risk register.

> Generic case studies don't cut it. You need proof from organisations of your size, in your sector.

### Next steps

Pair this with a clear **business case** that ties initiatives to measurable outcomes — that's the thread we pull through every engagement.
